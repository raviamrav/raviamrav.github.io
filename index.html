About me

I'm a software engineer with 25 years of experience — from BPO production floors to backend systems, from technical lead to full-stack applications I deploy and maintain myself.

Most of my professional career has been in backend development: Perl at Unitedprint for six years, and before that leading content-conversion systems for US legal publishers at Exela and SourceHOV. I've since built my own full-stack projects in Vue 3, React, FastAPI, and ASP.NET Core — all live, all on GitHub.

I use AI tools — Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Copilot — not because they are a trend, but because they align with my core belief: software and tools exist to provide business solutions. Anything that helps deliver better solutions faster belongs in the toolkit.

I'm settled in Riesa, Saxony with my family. Niederlassungserlaubnis. German B1 in progress. Looking for the right role — full-stack, backend, or lead — in Saxony or remote.

  • Why Germany: came for opportunity, stayed for family. Riesa is home — not a stepping stone.
  • What kind of role: a team where I can contribute technically and bring structure when needed. Not looking to manage only — I want to keep building.
